About us

Panther Foundation is an online crowdfunding platform that enables anyone across world to raise funds for healthcare, education, sports, disaster relief and other personal causes, with great ease.

We serve all children regardless of religion, race, ethnicity or gender as a demonstration of Christ’s unconditional love for all people.
